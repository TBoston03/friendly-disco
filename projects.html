Sand Drive Solutions Tablet

Senior Design Project - Working with classmates to develop a secure device for testing and securing drives before connecting them to a network.

We set out to build a portable lab environment that would allow cybersecurity professionals to test portable drives on the go. We intended to achieve this goal by developing an easy-to-use device with multiple input mediums, allowing testing of a wide variety of devices. The device will create a safe and secure environment, separate from any outside network, where we can perform several different tests for common forms of malware and present a report to the user, informing them of the condition of the device. The purpose of our product is to provide a convenient platform for testing drives in the field, eliminating the need to take them to a dedicated lab workstation.
